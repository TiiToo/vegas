Classpublic class MoneyBag
ImplementsIMoney

A MoneyBag is represented as a list of Monies and provides different constructors to create a MoneyBag. For example adding 12 euros to 14 US Dollars is represented as a bag containing the two Monies 12€ and 14$. Adding another 10 euros gives a bag with 22€ and 14$.
